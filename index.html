Homepage

Hi, I'm Stella Garner.

I am a senior journalism student studying at the University of Maryland. Minoring in Digital Storytelling and Poetics, I have elementary experience with coding and its practical applications within the world of journalism. I am the Tabloids Editor for The Diamondback, the university's oldest news outlet, and a General Assignment Editor for Stories Beneath the Shell, another campus paper.

I specialize in news writing pertaining to politics, arts and culture, and I have previously held internships in the United States Senate and at Baltimore Magazine. Outside of academics, I am a member of the University of Maryland Drumline and the Mighty Sound of Maryland marching band. I also am the percussion section leader for the university's Gamer Symphony Orchestra, the first collegiate orchestra whose repertoire is entirely video game music.

On the other links of this page, you will find my resume, clips and contact information. I look forward to hearing from you!
